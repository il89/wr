Our Story

Get to know our story

Highlight 1

April 2015
Rodrigo and Tania first laid eyes on one another in quite an unromantic location - the office of the company Rodrigo was working for and where Tania was going to start at. Interestingly enough, Rodrigo had already seen a photo of the new girl, and something was telling him that she might be the one.

Highlight 2

June 2015
After several weeks of hints and secret glances, Rodrigo and Tania had an opportunity to get to know each other better, and it was during an evening walk by the beach in June that something magical was about to flourish.

Highlight 3

September 2015
Tired of driving to Avilés to see Tania, Rodrigo offered her to move in with him in Gijón. It was perhaps a quick and risky move, but after assembling an Ikea wardrobe together without coming to blows, apart from a summer full of memories, they understood they were made for each other.

Highlight 4

January 2016
Tania and Rodrigo are forced to spend a couple of months apart: Rodrigo has an opportunity to start a new and promising work project in Barcelona, and Tania has to stay in Gijón, but only for a short amount of time.

Highlight 5

April 2016
After a brief period Tania and Rodrigo are together again, starting an exciting adventure in the city of Barcelona on the Mediterranean shore.

Highlight 6

June 2017
The journey to Paris, which oddly enough is Tania’s present to Rodrigo for his birthday to see the concert of his favourite band, ends up with a dinner opposite the Eiffel Tower, where Rodrigo makes the most important proposal of his life to Tania.

Highlight 7

8/09/2018
Tania is unable to resist, and they decide on the Day of Asturias for their wedding day, far away from Rodrigo’s homeland but in a place that is every bit as good - in the Sitges church, facing the sea, halfway point between Asturias and Latvia, Tania’s home country.

More Event Info

The first part of the wedding will be, of course, the ceremony in the church. It will take place at the Church of Sant Bartomeu i Santa Tecla in Sitges, and will begin at 5:30pm sharp. Sitges is located about 40km from Barcelona and can be reached by both car and train.

Please allow between 45 and 60 minutes travel time to reach Sitges if leaving from Barcelona (it’s awkward being late to a wedding, isn’t it?), since the traffic might get heavy on a Saturday in September - the weather still allows going to the beach (stop being envious :) If your accommodation is in Sitges, the church is right by the sea and it takes less than 15 minutes walking from basically anywhere in the town.

When the official part comes to an end, there will be buses waiting to take us to the restaurant La Cúpula del Garraf located in the town of Garraf, about 20 minutes from Sitges. The celebration will take place both inside and outside (large terrace), weather permitting, and girls, do not worry about your shoes since there is firm ground and no sand/grass whatsoever.

There will be plenty of delicious tapas and drinks so that you are full of energy and ready to dance the night away until 3:00am. When we are finished celebrating our special day, the buses will take you back to Sitges.

If you are joining us from out of town, you may wish to book a hotel or an Airbnb. Please bear in mind that there is an important local event taking place at the same time, so you might want to get your accommodation as soon as possible. For your information, we have reached an agreement with the Hotel Ibiza in the centre of Sitges, which will offer you an attractive price for both nights (Friday 7th and Saturday 8th) - if you are interested in this option just let us know for further details. Alternatively, you can look for other hotels or apartments in Sitges, or stay at nearby Casteldefels or Barcelona.

Any questions, comments, concerns, playlist requests, or accommodation inquiries, feel free to contact Rodrigo or Tania.
